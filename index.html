HCI Term Project

This is a website to host Google LLC's work for UGA's Fall 2020 Human-Computer Interaction term project.
Members: Henry Faulkner, Danny Kim, Kamiru Ndiragu, Matthew Hanagriff


For this semester, the theme of the term projects was remote work. Specifically, identifying a problem unique to remote work and how our group could attempt to solve or remediate it. Therefore, our group identified the issue of dealing with time management and distractions caused by remote work. More specifically, we believe that one of the biggest issues with time management unique to remote work is the fact that not being present at the workplace causes a lack of direct supervision. We think this could lead to being more prone to distractions (mainly online), and therefore directly contributing to workers being less productive. Thus, our group will attempt to remediate this issue by identifying types of time-tracking apps and/or interfaces and seeing how we can improve both their functionality, and how to mitigate the identified issues of remote work by attempting to discover how to refine their interfaces through improvements and analysis.
